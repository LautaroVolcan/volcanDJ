I am Lautaro Ferrandi, AKA LAUTARO VOLCAN, an Argentinian/Spanish DJ.

I am passionate about Tech House, House, Techno, Nu Disco, and Ambient music, and I focus on creating unique musical atmospheres that connect deeply with the audience. I love selecting tracks with groove, vocals, and rhythmic patterns, exploring their essence to build immersive sonic experiences that prioritize collective enjoyment.
As a DJ, I pride myself on my versatility and creativity, delivering innovative sets that blend a wide range of styles. I’ve had the opportunity to perform at clubs and events in different countries, adapting to diverse audiences and venues.
My interest in the Ambient genre allows me to incorporate introspective elements into my mixes, creating atmospheres filled with emotion and depth.
